About Us

Hotel Kanchan

Hotel Kanchan is a well-established lodging destination situated in the bustling South Tukoganj neighbourhood of Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Located on S P Marg near Gokuldas Hospital, our hotel has served guests from across the country for many years, offering round-the-clock availability and a range of amenities suited to both business and leisure travellers.

With over a thousand guest reviews and a steady presence in one of Indore's most accessible localities, Hotel Kanchan remains committed to providing decent, manageable accommodation at a convenient address. Whether you are visiting for medical purposes, business, or exploring the city, our 24-hour front desk ensures you always have a place to call home in Indore.
